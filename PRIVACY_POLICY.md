# Privacy Policy - Sleep Tight

**Last Updated:** February 15, 2026

At **GenAi Apps**, we believe the safety and privacy of children is of paramount importance. **Sleep Tight** is designed to be a fun, safe, and family-friendly experience for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

## 1. Our Commitment to Families
We strictly adhere to the **Google Play Families Policy**. This app:
- Does not contain inappropriate content.
- Does not collect personal information from children.
- Does not use any third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

## 2. Information Collection & Use
**Sleep Tight does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).**
- **No Registration:** You do not need to create an account or provide an email address.
- **Offline Play:** The game functions entirely offline.
- **Local Storage:** We only store high scores and game settings locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

## 3. Third-Party Services
This app does not use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. 
- No Ads.
- No Analytics.
- No Social Media integrations.

## 4. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)
We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions, contact us at: **support@genaiapps.com**
