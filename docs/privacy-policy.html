Sleep Tight

Privacy Policy | Last Updated: February 15, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Families

At GenAi Apps, we believe the safety and privacy of children is of paramount importance. Sleep Tight is designed to be a fun, safe, and family-friendly experience for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

Google Play Families Policy Compliance:
We strictly adhere to the Google Play Families Policy. This app does not contain inappropriate content, does not collect personal information from children, and does not use any third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

2. Information Collection & Use

Sleep Tight does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).

  • No Registration: You do not need to create an account or provide an email address to play.
  • Offline Play: The core game mechanics function entirely offline.
  • Local Storage: We only store high scores and game settings locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Specifically:

  • No Ads: There are no advertisements in the app.
  • No Analytics: We do not use tools like Google Analytics or Firebase to track user behavior.
  • No Social Media: There are no social media integrations or sharing features that could expose data.

4. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can take necessary actions to delete it.

5. Data Safety Commitment

In the Google Play Data Safety section, we declare that:

  • No data is shared with third parties.
  • No data is collected.
  • Data is encrypted in transit (though we don't transmit data).
  • Users can request data deletion (though no data is stored on servers).
